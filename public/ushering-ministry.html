Ushering Ministry

Creating a welcoming environment and ensuring smooth services through dedicated service.

First Impressions Matter

The Ushering Ministry is often the first point of contact for visitors and members alike. Our ushers create a warm, welcoming environment that helps people feel at home and prepares their hearts for worship.

Our Responsibilities

Greeting & Welcoming

Warmly welcome everyone as they arrive for services and events

Seating Assistance

Help attendees find seating and assist with special needs

Service Coordination

Ensure smooth flow of services and handle any emergencies

Offering Collection

Manage the offering collection with dignity and respect

Making People Feel Welcome

We believe that every person who walks through our doors should feel valued and welcomed. Our ushers are trained to be attentive, helpful, and ready to serve with a cheerful heart.

Join Our Team

If you enjoy serving people and have a welcoming personality, consider joining our ushering team. We provide training for all new ushers and work in rotating teams.
