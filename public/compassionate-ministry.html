Compassionate Ministry

Serving those in need through practical assistance, emotional support, and Christ's love.

Serving with Compassion

The Compassionate Ministry reaches out to people in our community who are facing difficult circumstances. We provide practical assistance, emotional support, and share the hope of Christ with those in need, following Jesus' example of compassion.

Our Programs

Food Assistance

Weekly food distribution and feeding programs for families in need

Clothing Ministry

Providing clothing and essential items to those in difficult situations

Medical Missions

Quarterly medical and dental missions in partnership with local health workers

Counseling Support

Emotional and spiritual support for those facing crises or difficult life situations

Community Impact

Each month, we serve over 200 families through our various outreach programs. Our volunteers provide not just material help, but also build relationships and share God's love through personal connections.

Get Involved

Whether you can donate resources, volunteer your time, or help with organizing events, there are many ways to serve in our Compassionate Ministry. Training and orientation are provided for all new volunteers.
