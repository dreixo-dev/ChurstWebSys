Guiding Our Church Family

Our pastoral team is dedicated to serving the Emmanuel Christian Church community with wisdom, compassion, and spiritual leadership. Meet the shepherds who guide our congregation in faith and service.

Rev. Laurence Gatawa

Senior Pastor

Rev. Laurence Gatawa, Ph.D. has served as Senior Pastor of Emmanuel Christian Church (ECC) since 2013. He also teaches at PTS College and Advanced Studies, the national seminary of the Presbyterian Church of the Philippines. He is passionate about pastoral ministry and training future church leaders.

Pstr. Armando Urbiztondos

Associate Pastor

He was trained in the field of evangelism in his conversion in Bohol and became a member of ECC in 2006. His ministry in youth, kids, as well as in music ministry, blessed the people in God's grace. His life testimony demonstrates God's forgiveness and mercy. He took AB Theology at Presbyterian Theological Seminary and Advanced Studies, Dasmariñas City, and is pursuing his MAED in Guidance and Counseling at DLSU-D while serving as a Guidance Designate and Values Education Department Head.

Pstr. Albert Gono

Youth Pastor

Pastor Albert Gono is a servant of God shaped by humble beginnings and His sustaining grace. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Theology (ABTh) from Presbyterian Theological Seminary (PTS) and has faithfully served the Lord for 3 years. At Emmanuel Christian Church, he is dedicated to leading the congregation through singing, teaching, and loving God’s people while trusting God to guide every step of his ministry.
