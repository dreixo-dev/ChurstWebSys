About Our Church

Emmanuel Christian Church is a community of believers committed to following Jesus Christ and sharing His love with the world.

Our Mission & Vision

At Emmanuel Christian Church, our mission is to make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God passionately and serve others selflessly. We envision a community transformed by the gospel, where every person experiences God's love and purpose.

Founded in 2005, our church has been serving the General Trias community for over 18 years. We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the importance of community in the Christian journey.

Our Faith

We believe in the saving grace of Jesus Christ.

Our Community
