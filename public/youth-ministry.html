Youth Ministry

Engaging teenagers and young adults in their faith journey through relevant programs and authentic community.

Next Generation for Christ

Our Youth Ministry creates a dynamic, safe space where young people can explore faith, build friendships, and grow spiritually. We're passionate about raising up the next generation of Christian leaders.

Youth Programs

Friday Night Live

Weekly youth gathering with worship, teaching, and fun activities

Game Nights & Events

Monthly fun events including games, movies, and outings

Youth Retreats

Annual camps and retreats for deeper spiritual growth

Service Projects

Opportunities to serve in church and community projects

Age Groups

We have programs for both Junior High (grades 7-10) and Senior High (grades 11-12) students, as well as a College & Young Adults group for those 18-25.

Safe & Fun Environment

All our youth leaders are trained and background-checked. We prioritize creating an environment where youth feel safe, valued, and free to be themselves while growing in their faith.
