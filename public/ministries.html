Our Ministries

Discover how you can serve, grow, and connect through our various ministry opportunities. Each ministry is designed to help you use your gifts and passions for God's glory.

Praise and Worship

Leading the congregation in heartfelt worship through music and arts. This ministry creates an atmosphere where people can encounter God through contemporary and traditional worship.

Learn More

Women's Ministry

Encouraging and equipping women to grow in their faith and serve others. Through Bible studies, retreats, and fellowship events, women find support and spiritual growth.

Learn More

Home Visitation Ministry

Visiting and ministering to church members and newcomers in their homes. This ministry provides pastoral care, prayer, and fellowship to those who need encouragement.

Learn More

Compassionate Ministry

Serving those in need through practical assistance and emotional support. This ministry reaches out to the community with food assistance, counseling, and practical help.

Learn More

Prayer Warriors

Interceding for the needs of our church, community, and world through prayer. This dedicated group meets regularly to pray for specific needs and God's guidance.

Learn More

Ushering Ministry

Welcoming and assisting attendees during our worship services and events. Ushers create a welcoming environment and help services run smoothly.

Learn More

Men's Ministry

Building strong Christian men through fellowship, discipleship, and service. Men gather for Bible study, accountability, and service projects in the community.

Learn More

Youth Ministry

Engaging teenagers and young adults in their faith journey through relevant programs. Youth experience dynamic worship, relevant teaching, and fun fellowship.

Learn More

Children Ministry

Nurturing children in faith through age-appropriate teaching and fun activities. Kids learn about God through interactive lessons, music, and creative play.

Learn More
