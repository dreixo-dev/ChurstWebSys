About Our Church

We want to share the Good News of the Lord, and extend the blessing of SALVATION in our community and reach as many people as possible, as commanded by our Lord Jesus Christ in "Matthew 28:19-20" Matthew 28:19-20 "Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things that I have commanded you; and lo, l am with you always, even to the end of the age." Amen.

Our Mission & Vision

Emmanuel Christian Church envisions being a worshipping community of believer in Christ which proclaims the Gospel in words and deed for the transformation of lives and society.

As a united body of Christ and established by the power of the Holy Spirit, ECC aims to glorify God through the following:

1. We EVANGELIZE the unsaved by proclaiming the Gospel.
2. We COVENANT to care for each member disciple for their spiritual edification.
3. We COMMIT ourselves to Christ-like living and works of service and worship.
